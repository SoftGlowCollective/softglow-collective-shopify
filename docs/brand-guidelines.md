## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) or similar elegant serif
  - Conveys luxury, confidence, and quality
  - Use for: H1, H2, H3 titles, hero text
  - Weight: 600-700 (semi-bold to bold)

- **Body**: Inter, Source Sans Pro, or similar modern sans-serif
  - Ensures readability and trust
  - Use for: Body text, descriptions, labels
  - Weight: 400-500 (regular to medium)

### Font Sizing
- **H1 (Hero)**: 48-56px
- **H2 (Section)**: 36-42px
- **H3 (Subsection)**: 24-28px
- **Body**: 16px
- **Small/Caption**: 12-14px

### Line Height
- Headings: 1.2-1.3
- Body: 1.6-1.8

## Imagery & Visual Style

### Product Photography
- **Style**: Clean, minimalist, luxury
- **Background**: White, soft textures, or subtle blurred
- **Lighting**: Bright, even, flattering
- **Angles**: Flat lay, 3/4 view, detail shots
- **Props**: Complementary minimalist props (gold, white, glass)

### Hero Images
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or full-screen
- **Focus**: Product in lifestyle context, aspirational
- **Tone**: Calming, luxurious, trustworthy

### Icons
- **Style**: Minimalist line icons
- **Colors**: Bluish Pink or Gold on white
- **Sizing**: Scale consistently for hierarchy

## Spacing & Layout

### Whitespace
- **Generous margins**: 40-60px between major sections
- **Padding**: 20-30px inside containers
- **Line spacing**: As per typography guidelines

### Grid
- **Desktop**: 12-column grid
- **Tablet**: 8-column grid
- **Mobile**: Full-width with safe margins (20px)

### Mobile Optimization
- Stack all elements vertically
- Increase touch target size (min 44px)
- Reduce padding/margins slightly for mobile

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Luxurious**: Premium quality, sophisticated language
- **Trustworthy**: Clear, honest, evidence-based claims
- **Aspirational**: Inspire confidence and wellness
- **Warm**: Approachable, relatable, not pretentious
- **Educational**: Help customers understand benefits

### Language Guidelines
- Use benefit-focused copy ("Radiant, glowing skin" vs. "Moisturizer")
- Emphasize quality, ingredients, sustainability
- Tell stories about self-care and wellness
- Avoid overly technical jargon; explain simply when needed

### Example Copy
- ✅ "Elevate your skincare ritual with our curated collection of luxury beauty essentials"
- ❌ "Buy skincare products here"

## Logo & Branding Assets

### Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size**: 200px width on desktop, 100px on mobile
- **Spacing**: Clear space around logo (2x the logo height)
- **Backgrounds**: Works on white, bluish pink, and light gold
- **Color Versions**: Full color, white (on colored backgrounds), dark

### Favicon
- Icon version of logo, bluish pink on white
- 32px, 16px, and 180px sizes for different platforms

## Component Guidelines

### Product Cards
- Image with hover zoom effect
- Product name (serif, bold)
- Price in gold
- Rating with stars
- "Add to Cart" button (primary style)
- Quick view on hover

### Trust Badges
- Fast Shipping (truck icon)
- Secure Checkout (lock icon)
- Quality Guaranteed (star icon)
- Use gold color for emphasis

### Newsletter Signup
- Headline: "Join Our Glow Community"
- Input field with gold border on focus
- Button: Primary bluish pink
- Confirmation message in gold text

### Customer Testimonials
- 5-star rating
- Customer name and photo
- Quote text (serif font)
- Gold stars
- Subtle background color (light cream)

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast
- Text on backgrounds: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AA)
- Interactive elements: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio
- Test with: WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Typography
- Font size minimum: 16px for body text
- Line height minimum: 1.6
- Line length maximum: 80 characters for readability

### Interactive Elements
- Focus indicators: Gold outline, 2-3px
- Hover states: Clear visual feedback
- Touch targets: Minimum 44x44px
